Zach Kondak is alright

Yeah sure, I'd say Zach is pretty alright. Zach is a composer, performer, and technologist based in Albany NY. Zach studied music technology within Gil Weinberg's Robotic Musicianship Group at Geotgia, and wound up getting a pretty cool master's degree there. Zach's research focused on endowing robotic musicians with the ability to interact with human performers (in real-time) in such a way as to lead an improvational performance.
